Paris, France

What the city offers

Paris is a beautiful city with many wonderful attractions and great cuisine. Don't forget to stop by any of their bakeries for some authentic French goods. Here are a few of my personal favorite locations to visit when I go to Paris:

  • Eiffel Tower
  • Disneyland Paris
  • Château de Versailles
  • Arc de Triomphe

Some interesting information about Paris:

  • Paris is the fashion capital of the world.
  • Paris is known for its café culture and world-renowned cuisine.
